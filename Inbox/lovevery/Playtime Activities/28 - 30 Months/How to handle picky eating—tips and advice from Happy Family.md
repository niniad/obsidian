### What makes our kids picky eaters?

* Toddlers‚Äô growth rate slows down from when they were infants, and often so does their appetite.
* Becoming weary of new foods is believed to be an evolutionary benefit for young children to protect them from eating potentially poisonous plants. In modern days, this can be a barrier to trying new and safe foods.
* Young children need and love snacks and drinks. However, too much of these can crowd out meals, especially if poorly timed.
* Children and people all interpret sensory stimuli differently. Children with strong sensory responses may experience taste and smell more strongly, making them more sensitive to foods and thus ‚Äúpicky eaters.‚Äù
