### Long and short

![Woman holding a toddler in their lap and putting a piece of ribbon around their wrist](https://i0.wp.com/blog.lovevery.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Weekly_Week-81-Body-2.jpg?resize=1176%2C649&ssl=1)
