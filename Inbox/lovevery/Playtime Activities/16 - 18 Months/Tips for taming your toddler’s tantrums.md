## Why toddler tantrums happen (hint: it isn‚Äôt about you)

Your toddler doesn‚Äôt yet have the skills to handle all of their big emotions. Sometimes they need to get those big feelings out in a big way.

#### Tantrums can‚Äôt be stopped in the moment
