**1. They don‚Äôt understand how their actions affect others.** Your toddler is just beginning to realize that they are their own person. This is the precursor to empathy, which takes years to develop.

### What can you do about toddler kicking, biting, and hitting?

### Why timeouts don‚Äôt work‚Äîand what to do instead

Timeouts are a controversial topic. They were first suggested as an alternative to spanking, but in the decades since, their effectiveness has come into question. * They don‚Äôt learn how to understand their emotions or what to do with them

> ‚ÄúHow can I help my toddler understand that hitting isn‚Äôt okay?‚Äù
