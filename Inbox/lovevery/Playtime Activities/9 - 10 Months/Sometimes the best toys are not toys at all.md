## Here are some tips for ways to engage with your baby in the tasks of everyday life:

* Tap on a window or door when you are holding your baby and see how they tune into the sound.
* Let your baby (supervised) dump tea bags out of the box and explore them.
* Your baby is working their fine motor skills. With more and more practice, they will be able to turn things on and off on their own. Until then, put your fingers over theirs while you manipulate switches and faucets and door handles together.
