Petlibro (ペトリブロ) is a brand that designs and sells smart pet products, such as Wi‑Fi‑enabled automatic feeders, water fountains, and pet cameras. Founded in 2019, the company focuses on improving the bond between pets and their owners through connected, app‑controlled devices.
