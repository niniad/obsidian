The Looker Play Kit is a newborn development kit from Lovevery for babies aged 0‑12 weeks. It includes a high‑contrast mobile, sensory links, simple and complex black‑and‑white card sets, a standing card holder, black‑and‑white mittens, a wooden book, and a silicone rattle. The kit is designed to help newborns build brain connections, strengthen vision, and develop body awareness through open‑ended, high‑quality play.
