Dr. Jennifer Cross is a child behavioral expert and an Attending Pediatrician at NewYork-Presbyterian Phyllis and David Komansky Children's Hospital at Weill Cornell Medical Center. She is an expert in the diagnosis and management of children with developmental disabilities and is board‑certified in Developmental and Behavioral Pediatrics. She frequently comments on screen‑time guidelines for children.
