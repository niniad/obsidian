Mideer (弥鹿) is a Chinese toy brand owned by Ningbo Mideer Network Technology Co., Ltd. The brand focuses on early childhood education and artistic development, offering products such as artistic puzzles, STEAM toys, educational board games, and wearable nail stickers. Its products are sold globally in over 70 countries.
