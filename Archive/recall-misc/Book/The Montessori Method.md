The Montessori Method is Maria Montessori’s 1912 book (originally published in Italian in 1909 as *Il Metodo della Pedagogia Scientifica*) that outlines her scientific pedagogy and the principles of the Montessori educational approach. It introduced the method to an American audience and has been widely translated and influential in education.
