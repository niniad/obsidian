The Subjective Happiness Scale (SHS) is a 4-item self‑report measure of individual subjective happiness. It assesses global subjective happiness with two items asking respondents to characterize themselves using absolute ratings and ratings relative to peers, and two items offering brief descriptions of happy and unhappy individuals.
