The Piers–Harris Children’s Self-Concept Scale is a self‑report questionnaire for children ages 7‑18 that measures overall self‑concept and six domain subscales (Behavioral Adjustment, Intellectual and School Status, Physical Appearance and Attributes, Freedom from Anxiety, Popularity, and Happiness & Satisfaction). It is published by Western Psychological Services.
