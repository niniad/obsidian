The Warwick‑Edinburgh Mental Well‑being Scale (WEMWBS) is a 14‑item questionnaire designed to measure positive mental health, covering both hedonic and eudaimonic aspects. It is widely used in research and public‑health settings to assess mental well‑being in the general population and specific groups.
