ECAI is a marketing automation tool that extends LINE Official Account functionality, offering features such as segmented and step messaging, automated customer responses, analytics, and integration with e‑commerce platforms (Shopify, ECForce, etc.). It is marketed in Japan for LINE‑based marketing automation.
