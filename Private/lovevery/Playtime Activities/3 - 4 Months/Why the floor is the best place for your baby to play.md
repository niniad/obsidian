# Why the floor is the best place for your baby to play

Source: https://blog.lovevery.com/child-development/why-the-floor-is-the-best-place-for-your-baby-to-play/

![Baby laying in The Play Gym by Lovevery](https://i0.wp.com/blog.lovevery.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/2021_03_18_General_Marketing4467.jpg?fit=2048%2C1214&ssl=1)

The floor is a perfect place for your baby to play. It may seem obvious, but many experts recommend babies spend more time on the floor—and less time in seats, swings, and strollers—to build muscle strength and control.
