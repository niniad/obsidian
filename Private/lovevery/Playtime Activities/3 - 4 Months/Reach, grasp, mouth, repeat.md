Somewhere between 2 ¬Ω and 3 ¬Ω months, your baby‚Äôs hands will start to open up. If their hands are mostly in a fist, here are some ideas to help your baby open their hands:

* Stroke the back of their hands and see if they open their fist
* Gently uncurl their fingers and thumb
* Rub or pat their hands together, or pat their hand on your hand
* Let them touch and pat your face

### Reflexive grasping

![Baby laying down grabbing on to a woman's finger](https://i0.wp.com/blog.lovevery.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/Marcella-8254_VS_R1.jpg?resize=1200%2C800&ssl=1)

Before about 4 months, your baby‚Äôs fingers will reflexively wrap around an object when it is placed in their hand. The more often you engage your baby‚Äôs grasping reflex, the sooner they‚Äôll be able to open and close their hands voluntarily. Here‚Äôs how you can help:

* Stroke the back of your baby‚Äôs hands to help open them
* Place a [ring](https://lovevery.com/products/the-play-kits-the-charmer) inside their hands; they‚Äôll reflexively wrap their fingers around it
* Help them keep grasping the ring by gently putting your hands over their fist
