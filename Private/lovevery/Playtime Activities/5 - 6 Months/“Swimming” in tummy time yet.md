### If your baby is pushing up on their arms, but not yet pivoting and moving in circles when they‚Äôre on their tummy (the stage known as ‚Äúswimming‚Äù), here are some ways to play:

![Illustration of a baby doing tummy time and lifting their chest up](https://i0.wp.com/blog.lovevery.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Weekly_Week-19-Body-2.jpg?resize=1176%2C784&ssl=1)

* Start by putting your baby in a short-sleeved onesie on a smooth floor (as opposed to a rug or blanket) so they can use their skin against the floor to get a little grip.
* Place them on their belly with [toys](https://lovevery.com/pages/tummy-time-toys) in a semi-circle around them,¬†just within reach when they pivot. Move the toys a little farther away after some play time to encourage more movement.
