## Here are some fun ways for your toddler to explore glue:

### **Dot-to-dot pom poms**

* Find some large pom poms in the same colors as your dots.
* Give your child the pom poms and ask them to match the color of the pom pom with the color of the sticker or dot.
* Using a small bowl of liquid glue and a brush, your child can glue each pom pom to its matching dot.

### **Sand or salt art**
