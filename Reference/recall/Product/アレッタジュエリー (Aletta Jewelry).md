Aletta Jewelry is a Japanese luxury jewelry brand known for its clean and minimalist design, high-quality silver accessories, and gold jewelry. The brand was established in 2019 and is committed to sustainability through thorough recycling of materials and handcrafted production by skilled craftsmen.
