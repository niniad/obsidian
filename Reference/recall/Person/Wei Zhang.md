Wei Zhang is a researcher at the Surrey Institute of Education, University of Surrey, focusing on the well-being of higher vocational education students in China. Another Wei Zhang is a Professor of Energy Technology at the Advanced Technology Institute, University of Surrey, with research interests including halide perovskites for photovoltaic and light-emission applications. A third Wei Zhang is a PhD researcher in Translation and Interpreting Studies at the Centre for Translation Studies (CTS), University of Surrey. Finally, a fourth Wei Zhang is an Associate Professor in Education at the University of Leicester.
