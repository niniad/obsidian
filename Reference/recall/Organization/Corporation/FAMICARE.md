FAMICARE is a Japanese brand that designs and sells mother’s backpacks and baby bags (e.g., the "SUNNY BABY LOVE WITH CARE" series). The products feature USB charging ports, night‑reflection tags, and secure pockets, and are sold through Japanese e‑commerce platforms such as Rakuten.
