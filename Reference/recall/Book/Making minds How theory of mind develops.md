Making Minds: How Theory of Mind Develops is a 2014 book by Henry M. Wellman, published by Oxford University Press. The book provides a comprehensive overview of how children's theory of mind develops, covering belief‑desire understanding, social cognition, neural bases, cultural influences, and related topics.
