The Oxford Happiness Inventory (OHI) is a self‑report questionnaire consisting of 29 multiple‑choice items that assess various aspects of psychological well‑being, including satisfaction with life, mastery and self‑fulfillment, social cheerfulness, vigor, and social interest.
