The Subjective Happiness Scale (SHS) is a 4‑item self‑report questionnaire that measures global subjective happiness. It includes two absolute self‑rating items, two items comparing oneself to peers, and two items describing happy and unhappy persons, with responses on a 7‑point Likert scale.
