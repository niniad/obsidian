The 赤ちゃん防災プロジェクト (Japan Protect Baby in Disaster Project) is an initiative by the Japan Dietetic Association‑Disaster Assistance Team (公益社団法人日本栄養士会) that provides nutritional guidance and support for infants and young children during disasters.
